Participate in Research


Our lab studies how blindness and visual experience influence the mind and brain. We have many different studies. Some involve behavioral testing, others functional MRI and still others use Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation.

We are always recruiting participants and grateful to anyone willing to take part in the research. If you are interested in participating in our studies, contact us at plasticity.lab2@gmail.com, or at (410) 870-9895. Potential blind participants have the option to set up a 30-minute phone screening for which they are compensated with a $15 Amazon Gift Card.
